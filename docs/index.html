Hover & select lookup

Point at a character to read it, or select a word for the whole-word reading and a character-by-character breakdown. Simplified & traditional.

Tone-colored pinyin

Stroke order

Reader mode

Open any article in a clean, distraction-free reader with selectable fonts, larger text, and optional pinyin above every character — dictionary still live.

⭐

Saved deck

Star words to build your own study list. It stays on your device.

Search anything

By hanzi, pinyin (tone marks optional), or English — ranked so common and HSK words come first.
